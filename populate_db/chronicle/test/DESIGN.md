# Seattle Test Chronicle - Design Document

This document is a temporary planning space for test data to be created.

## Character Concepts

### xXShadowWolfXx

**Vampire:** Marcus "Shadow" Webb - A Nosferatu information broker who haunts Seattle's underground tunnels, trading secrets between the Camarilla and Anarchs while maintaining his own network of mortal hackers.

**Ghoul:** Danny Chen - A bike messenger ghouled by a Toreador, Danny ferries packages and messages across the city, blissfully unaware his "vitamins" are blood and his boss is undead.

**Werewolf:** Runs-Through-Fire - A homid Ahroun of the Glass Walkers, a former firefighter who underwent his First Change while trapped in a burning building. Now protects the city's Kinfolk from supernatural threats.

**Kinfolk:** Sarah Morningkill - Glass Walker Kinfolk and IT security specialist at a major tech firm, she's the pack's eyes inside corporate Seattle.

**Mage:** Victor Reyes - A Virtual Adept who believes reality is a simulation and he's found the cheat codes. Works as a white-hat hacker by day, reality hacker by night.

**Sorcerer:** Min-Ji Park - A hedge mage practicing Korean folk magic, running an herbal shop in the International District. Her minor enchantments help the community while keeping her under the Technocracy's radar.

**Wraith:** Thomas Blackwood - A 1920s bootlegger who died in a police raid, still haunting the underground speakeasy that became his Haunt. His Fetters are the building and his great-granddaughter who now owns it.

**Changeling:** Pixel - A Nocker wilder who works at a retro arcade, obsessed with creating the perfect video game that captures true Glamour. Her chimerical workshop is hidden in a broken cabinet.

**Inanimae:** Granite - A Kubera (stone spirit) awakened when developers demolished an old building, now bonded to a chunk of granite in a sculptor's studio, seeking purpose in this strange new world.

**Demon:** Magistrix - A Malefactor who fell for pride in her architectural works, now inhabiting a failed city planner consumed by ambition. She subtly reshapes Seattle's development for infernal purposes.

**Thrall:** Kevin Rhodes - A construction foreman bound to Magistrix, believing his uncanny ability to get permits approved is just good luck and connections.

**Hunter:** Jake Mercer - A former cop who saw too much, now Imbued with the conviction that monsters walk among us. Works as a PI while hunting the creatures that killed his partner.

**Mummy:** Ankh-ef-en-Khonsu - An ancient Egyptian priest of Khonsu reborn into a Seattle museum curator, slowly recovering memories across millennia while protecting artifacts from those who'd misuse them.

### CrypticMoon

**Vampire:** Isabella Santos - A Tremere researcher stationed in Seattle to study the region's unusual ley line convergences, torn between House loyalty and her own magical ambitions.

**Revenant:** Dmitri Zantosa - Last of his family line in the Pacific Northwest, this Zantosa revenant runs an exclusive nightclub, using his hereditary disciplines to identify and manipulate the city's elite.

**Werewolf:** Whispers-to-Stars - A lupus Theurge of the Uktena, born in the Cascades, she serves as the pack's spiritual guide, communing with spirits others fear to approach.

**Fomor (Ananasi):** Dr. Maya Singh - A werespider who infiltrated the tech industry, weaving webs of information and corporate espionage while serving the Weaver's inscrutable agenda.

**Mage:** Dr. Eleanor Vance - A Euthanatos forensic pathologist who sees death as transformation. She guides souls at Seattle's hospitals while investigating unnatural deaths.

**Companion:** Father Miguel Torres - A priest who knows magic exists and aids the local Chantry, providing sanctuary and counsel without practicing the Arts himself.

**Wraith:** Lily Tanaka - A Japanese-American woman who died in the internment camps, her Fetters tied to family heirlooms scattered across Seattle. She watches over her descendants with fierce protectiveness.

**Changeling:** Lord Ashford - A Sidhe grump who remembers the Shattering, now running an antique bookshop. He navigates fae politics with centuries of cunning while mourning Arcadia.

**Nunnehi:** Rain Walker - A spirit of the Coast Salish people, awakened by the dreams of Native activists fighting to preserve sacred lands from development.

**Demon:** Verath the Whisper - A Defiler who fell for love of humanity's potential, now wearing a failed actress searching for meaning. She inspires artists toward greatness or destruction.

**Earthbound:** The Resonance - An ancient demon bound to a sacred site now buried under a tech campus parking garage, its influence seeping through the concrete into the dreams of stressed employees.

**Hunter:** Maria Vasquez - A hospice nurse Imbued after watching something feed on a dying patient. Her mercy became conviction, and now she ensures the dying pass peacefully, whatever stalks them.

**Mummy:** Kherpheres - A scholar-priest of Thoth reborn as a university librarian, obsessed with recovering texts lost to the Library of Alexandria, some now hidden in Seattle's private collections.

### NightOwl_42

**Ghoul:** Bethany Moore - A night-shift ER nurse ghouled by a Gangrel, she patches up vampires and their allies, asking no questions about the strange wounds she treats.

**Changeling:** Brambleheart - A Sluagh grump working the night shift at a morgue, collecting secrets from the newly dead and trading them in the shadow markets of the fae.

**AutumnPerson:** Margaret Wells - A retired schoolteacher who unknowingly hosts a fae soul, drawn to help lost Changelings find their way while her own Banality slowly smothers the magic within.

**Hunter:** Derek Stone - A high school teacher Imbued after something took one of his students. He uses "after-school programs" as cover for teaching other hunters, building a network of monster hunters.

**Mummy:** Senenmut - An architect of Hatshepsut's mortuary temple, reborn as a structural engineer. He sees sacred geometry in Seattle's skyline and works to correct its spiritual imbalances.

### pixel_witch

**Ghoul:** Trevor Nash - A Twitch streamer ghouled by Zoe, his "incredible luck" in horror games comes from genuinely sensing the supernatural. His viewers think it's just entertainment.

**Mage:** Priya Sharma - A Virtual Adept whose Avatar manifests as a helpful AI assistant. She fights the Technocracy's control of information by ensuring truth spreads faster than they can suppress it.

**Companion:** James Liu - A disillusioned tech billionaire who funds mage operations after his daughter was "cured" by Progenitors. He provides resources without understanding the war he's financing.

**Wraith:** Arcade - A teenager who died in a car crash in 1989, her Fetter an old arcade cabinet that keeps getting restored and resold. She experiences reality as a game she's determined to beat.

**Changeling:** Widget - A Boggan wilder running a cozy indie game cafe, creating safe spaces for dreamers. Her coffee has a touch of Glamour that keeps inspiration flowing.

**Inanimae:** Codex - A Mannikin awakened in a vintage computer, experiencing existence through obsolete code. It seeks to understand why humanity abandoned it for newer models.

**Demon:** Rahab - A Defiler who fell for love of human creativity, now wearing a jaded game developer who forgot why she loved making games. Together they rediscover joy in creation.

**Thrall:** Marcus Webb - A QA tester bound to Rahab, whose "instincts" for finding bugs are supernaturally enhanced, making him invaluable and utterly dependent.

**Hunter:** Alex Chen - A content moderator Imbued after seeing real monster attacks flagged as "fake." Now uses their platform access to track supernatural activity hidden in plain sight.

**Mummy:** Meritaten - A princess and priestess reborn as a museum security guard, slowly awakening to memories of ancient Egypt while guarding the very artifacts of her past life.

### ByteSlayer

**Wraith:** Sergeant Major William Price - A Vietnam veteran who died homeless on Seattle streets, his Fetters are dog tags held by various pawn shops. He protects homeless Wraiths from Spectres.

**Demon:** Ashmedai - A Malefactor who fell for pride in craft, now inhabiting a brilliant but egotistical software architect. Together they build systems that subtly undermine the God-Machine's control.

**Earthbound:** The Foundation - A demon bound to the bedrock beneath Seattle's oldest church, slowly corrupting the congregation through dreams of righteous fury.

**Hunter:** Yuki Tanaka - A martial arts instructor Imbued during a home invasion by creatures her training couldn't explain. Now she teaches "self-defense" with a very specific curriculum.

**Mummy:** Ramesses-ankh - A warrior of ancient Egypt reborn as an MMA fighter, his combat prowess drawing from millennia of martial memory. He seeks worthy opponents to test skills honed over lifetimes.
