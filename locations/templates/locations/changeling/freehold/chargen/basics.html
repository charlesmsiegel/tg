About Freeholds

Freeholds are constructed places between the Dreaming and the Autumn World where changelings work, socialize, hold court, and take refuge from Banality. They are typically built atop Glades and are held by freeholders who have bonded their souls to the balefire.

Aspect: This is the underlying dream or story of your freehold. It could be tied to the location's history, the emotions it evokes, or the legends associated with it. For example: "birthplace of chimerical creatures," "ancient font of memories," or "sexy nightclub with hidden primal darkness."
