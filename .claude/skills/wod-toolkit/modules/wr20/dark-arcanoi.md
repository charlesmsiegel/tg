## What are Dark Arcanoi?

Dark Arcanoi are:
- Corrupted or inverted versions of standard Arcanoi
- Powers unique to Spectres and the Shadow-consumed
- Fueled by Angst rather than Pathos
- Often destructive, corrupting, or reality-warping

---

## Accessing Dark Arcanoi

### Who Can Use Them
| User | Access |
|------|--------|
| Spectres | Full access |
| Wraiths in Catharsis | Shadow may use if known |
| Doomslayers | Limited, dangerous |
| Corrupted Wraiths | Rare, story-specific |

### Learning Dark Arcanoi
- Taught by Spectres
- Discovered in the Labyrinth
- Spontaneous manifestation during Catharsis
- **Warning**: Learning Dark Arcanoi increases permanent Angst

## Using Dark Arcanoi

### Activation
- Cost: Angst (not Pathos)
- May require Willpower
- Often have side effects

### Side Effects
| Effect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Angst Gain** | Using powers generates more Angst |
| **Corruption** | Visible marks of Oblivion |
| **Detection** | Attracts Spectres and Doomslayers |
| **Addiction** | Becomes easier, more tempting |

### For Wraiths
- Each use generates 1+ temporary Angst
- Permanent Angst may increase over time
- Risk of drawing Shadow's attention
- Can lead to Harrowing or Catharsis

## Shadecraft

Higher-level Spectre powers for manipulating Shadows directly:

### Shadow Call
Communicate with other wraiths' Shadows.

### Shadow Seed
Plant suggestions in a Shadow.

### Shadow Awakening
Strengthen a target's Shadow, potentially triggering Catharsis.

### Shadow Possession
Temporarily take control of another's Shadow.

### Shadow Manifestation
Give a Shadow temporary physical form.
