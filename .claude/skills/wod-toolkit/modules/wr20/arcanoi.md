## What are Arcanoi?

Arcanoi (singular: Arcanos) are the ghostly powers wraiths develop after death. They allow wraiths to:
- Interact with the Skinlands
- Travel through the Underworld
- Manipulate plasm and souls
- Affect the living and the dead

---

## Common vs Initiate Powers

| Type | Access | Learning |
|------|--------|----------|
| **Common** | Any wraith | Standard XP cost |
| **Initiate** | Guild members | Requires Guild Background |

Most Arcanoi have both Common powers (available to all) and Initiate powers (Guild secrets).

## Using Arcanoi

### Activation
- Most require Pathos expenditure
- Some require Willpower
- Some have ongoing costs

### Dice Pools
- Typically Attribute + Arcanos rating
- Difficulties usually 6-8
- Modified by circumstances

### Botches
- Can generate Angst
- May alert Spectres
- Other negative effects
