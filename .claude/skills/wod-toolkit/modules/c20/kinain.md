# Kinain Module

Create Kinain characters for C20—mortals with faerie blood who can perceive and interact with the Dreaming.

## What are Kinain?

Kinain are mortal descendants of changelings—humans with faerie ancestry. They cannot undergo Chrysalis but possess limited fae abilities:

- Can see through the Mists
- May use Kenning to perceive Glamour
- Some can use limited faerie Arts
- Immune to Enchantment (already touched by Glamour)
- Age normally, can have mortal lives

## Kinain vs. Kithain

| Aspect | Kinain | Kithain |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Nature | Mortal with fae blood | Fae soul in mortal body |
| Perception | See through Mists | Full fae sight |
| Magic | Limited (Fae Gifts) | Full Arts |
| Glamour | Limited pool | Full pool |
| Banality | Affects them | Core threat |
| Aging | Normal | Slowed in freeholds |
| Death | Final | Reincarnation |

## Creation Steps

1. **Concept** — Mortal identity, fae heritage
2. **Fae Heritage** — Which kith ancestry (affects abilities)
3. **Attributes** — 6/4/3 across Physical/Social/Mental
4. **Abilities** — 11/7/4 across Talents/Skills/Knowledges (cap 3)
5. **Backgrounds** — 5 dots
6. **Fae Gifts** — 3 dots (see below)
7. **Glamour** — 1-3 based on heritage
8. **Willpower** — 3
9. **Banality** — 5
10. **Merits & Flaws** — From character list + Kinain-specific
11. **Freebies** — 21
12. **Description** — Mortal appearance, fae touches
13. **Validate**

## Fae Gifts

Kinain spend 3 dots on Fae Gifts—their connection to the Dreaming.

### Gift: Faerie Sight (1-3 dots)
Ability to perceive the Dreaming:
| Dots | Perception |
|------|------------|
| 1 | Sense Glamour nearby |
| 2 | See chimerical objects/creatures |
| 3 | See fae miens, full faerie sight |

### Gift: Faerie Art (2-3 dots)
Access to limited cantrip magic:
| Dots | Access |
|------|--------|
| 2 | 1 Art at level 1-2, 2 Realm dots |
| 3 | 1 Art at level 1-3, 3 Realm dots |

**Limitation**: Kinain can never exceed Art 3 or Realm 3.

### Gift: Fae Touch (1 dot)
Can touch and affect chimerical objects physically.

### Gift: Dreaming Traveler (2 dots)
Can enter the Near Dreaming through trods (with guide).

### Gift: Glamour Sensitivity (1 dot)
Automatic Kenning—always aware of Glamour in area.

### Gift: Birthrights (2-3 dots)
Access to partial kith Birthrights:
| Dots | Birthright Access |
|------|-------------------|
| 2 | One Birthright at half effect |
| 3 | One Birthright at full effect |

**Note**: Does NOT include Frailty.

## Kinain Backgrounds

Most backgrounds work as normal. Special notes:

| Background | Notes |
|------------|-------|
| Chimera | Rare; requires Fae Touch or Faerie Sight 2+ |
| Dreamers | Kinain themselves may BE Dreamers |
| Holdings | Usually guest access only |
| Mentor | Often a changeling relative |
| Treasure | Rare; difficult to use |

### Kinain-Specific Backgrounds

**Fae Blood** (1-3 dots): Strength of faerie heritage
- Affects Glamour pool maximum
- Higher = stronger fae traits

## Kinain Glamour

### Maximum Glamour
| Fae Blood | Max Glamour |
|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | 3 |
| 2 | 5 |
| 3 | 7 |

### Regaining Glamour
Kinain regain Glamour through:
- Epiphany (like changelings, but harder)
- Being near balefires
- Contact with powerful fae
- Their own creative acts (if Dreamer)

## Kinain and Society

### Among Mortals
- Live normal human lives
- May be artists, dreamers, eccentrics
- Often drawn to fae-touched places
- Can serve as bridges between worlds

### Among Kithain
- Valued as allies and agents
- Can interact with mortal world freely
- Sometimes treated as lesser
- Oaths bind them like changelings

### Common Roles
| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Herald | Messenger between fae and mortal |
| Guardian | Protects mortal-side freehold |
| Dreamer | Provides Glamour |
| Agent | Acts in mortal world |
| Companion | Loyal friend to changeling |
