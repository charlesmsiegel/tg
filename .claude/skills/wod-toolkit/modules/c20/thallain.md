# Thallain Module

Create Thallain characters for C20—the dark fae, servants of nightmare and the Fomorians.

## What are Thallain?

Thallain are dark fae born from nightmare and fear, created by the Fomorians during the ancient wars. Unlike Kithain, they feed on terror, pain, and negative emotions. They returned in force during the Evanescence (2001) and now threaten changeling society.

## Thallain vs. Kithain

| Aspect | Kithain | Thallain |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Origin | Dreams, creativity | Nightmares, fear |
| Glamour Source | Inspiration, beauty | Terror, pain, suffering |
| Court | Seelie/Unseelie | Shadow Court / Black Court |
| Legacy | Seelie + Unseelie | Nightmare + Kithain |
| Banality | Destroys them | They can invoke it |
| Iron | Weakness | Less affected (some) |

## Thallain Character Creation

### Differences from Kithain

| Category | Kithain | Thallain |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Attributes | 7/5/3 | 7/5/3 |
| Abilities | 13/9/5 | 11/7/4 |
| Backgrounds | 5 | 5 |
| Arts | 3 | 3 |
| Realms | 5 | 5 |
| Glamour | By kith | By kith |
| Willpower | By kith | By kith |
| Banality | By seeming | 3 (all) |
| Nightmare | 0 | 3 (starting) |
| Freebies | 15 | 15 |

### Nightmare Trait

Thallain have **Nightmare** instead of following normal Banality rules.
- **Nightmare** fuels dark powers
- High Nightmare = more monstrous, harder to control
- Can be spent like Willpower for dark purposes

### Nightmare Legacies

Thallain have a **Nightmare Legacy** (primary) and a **Kithain Legacy** (secondary, usually Unseelie).

| Nightmare Legacy | Urge | Taboo |
|------------------|------|-------|
| **Deceiver** | Trick mortals/fae into harm | Being honest when lies would benefit |
| **Destroyer** | Break beautiful things | Creating or preserving beauty |
| **Devourer** | Consume (flesh, dreams, Glamour) | Sharing or giving |
| **Lurker** | Hide and observe prey | Revealing yourself unnecessarily |
| **Panderer** | Corrupt the innocent | Protecting innocence |
| **Perturbed** | Spread fear and unease | Creating comfort |
| **Ravager** | Violently attack | Showing mercy |
| **Tempter** | Lead others to their doom | Warning of danger |

## Creation Steps

1. **Concept** — Thallain kith, role, motivation
2. **Nightmare Legacy** — Primary Legacy
3. **Kithain Legacy** — Secondary (usually Unseelie)
4. **Attributes** — 7/5/3 across Physical/Social/Mental
5. **Abilities** — 11/7/4 across Talents/Skills/Knowledges (cap 3)
6. **Backgrounds** — 5 dots (limited selection)
7. **Arts** — 3 dots
8. **Realms** — 5 dots
9. **Glamour** — Base by kith
10. **Willpower** — Base by kith
11. **Nightmare** — Starting 3
12. **Birthrights & Frailty** — From kith
13. **Merits & Flaws** — Limited selection
14. **Freebies** — 15
15. **Description** — Monstrous appearance
16. **Validate**

## Antithesis

Each Thallain has an **Antithesis**—a specific thing that weakens or harms them beyond normal.

| Example Antitheses | Effect |
|-------------------|--------|
| Laughter | Causes pain, flees |
| Religious symbols | Cannot approach |
| Specific substance | Allergic reaction |
| Bright light | Weakened |
| Running water | Cannot cross |
| True name spoken | Compelled to obey |

## Ravaging & Musing

Thallain gather Glamour differently than Kithain.

### Ravaging (Primary Method)
Taking Glamour forcefully through fear, pain, or destruction:
- Terrorize mortals
- Corrupt Dreamers
- Destroy beautiful things
- Torture for Glamour

### Dark Rapture
Some Thallain can achieve twisted inspiration from:
- Witnessing suffering
- Participating in evil acts
- Corrupting innocence
