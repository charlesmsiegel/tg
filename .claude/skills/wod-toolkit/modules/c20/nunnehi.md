# Nunnehi Module

Create Nunnehi characters for C20—the Native American fae, children of the spirit world.

## What are Nunnehi?

Nunnehi are the fae of the Americas, born from the dreams and beliefs of Native American peoples. They have a unique connection to the spirit world and follow different traditions than the European Kithain.

## Nunnehi vs. Kithain

| Aspect | Nunnehi | Kithain |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Origin | Native American traditions | European traditions |
| Spirit World | Deep connection, can step sideways | Limited access |
| Courts | Summer/Winter (balance-focused) | Seelie/Unseelie (opposition) |
| Magic | Medicine (similar to Arts) | Arts |
| Organization | Tribal, consensus-based | Feudal hierarchy |

## Nunnehi Courts

Unlike Kithain Seelie/Unseelie, Nunnehi follow **Summer** and **Winter** camps:

| Camp | Season | Philosophy |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Summer** | Growth, warmth | Active engagement, helping mortals |
| **Winter** | Dormancy, cold | Withdrawal, focusing on spirit world |

### Camp Mechanics
- Less adversarial than Kithain courts
- Seasonal shifts may change camp
- Both camps valued equally
- Balance is the goal

## Medicine (Nunnehi Arts)

Nunnehi use **Medicine** instead of Arts. Most Kithain Arts have Nunnehi equivalents:

| Kithain Art | Nunnehi Medicine | Notes |
|-------------|------------------|-------|
| Chicanery | Chicanery | Same |
| Primal | Primal | Stronger for Nunnehi |
| Soothsay | Soothsay | Same |
| Wayfare | Wayfare | Includes spirit walking |
| Dream-Craft | Dream-Craft | Central to traditions |

### Unique Nunnehi Medicine
- **Spirit Link**: Direct communication with spirits, possession
- Access to Umbra/spirit world easier than Kithain
