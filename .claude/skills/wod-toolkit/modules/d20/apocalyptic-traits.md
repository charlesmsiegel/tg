# Apocalyptic Traits Reference

Complete catalog of all 21 Visages with their exact 4 low-torment and 4 high-torment abilities from the source material.

**Important**: Each demon has exactly ONE visage, determined by their primary Lore. Each visage grants exactly 4 low-torment abilities OR 4 high-torment abilities (never both at once), depending on the demon's current Torment score.

---

## Common Lore Visage

### Bel — Visage of the Celestials

**Lore**: Celestials | **Associated Houses**: All (Common Lore)

**Low-Torment Appearance**: A luminous, lordly angel radiating divine grandeur and authority. Skin glows with golden light that shifts in intensity with mood. Eyes blaze with cold starlight. Seems to tower over everyone despite actual size.

**Low-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Wings**: Eagle's wings extend from shoulders. Glide up to 3x running speed per turn.
2. **Lordly Mien**: Aura of divine authority. -2 difficulty to Charisma and Manipulation rolls.
3. **Enhanced Senses**: Five senses heightened to superhuman levels. -2 difficulty to Perception rolls.
4. **Increased Awareness**: Especially attuned to reality's fabric. -2 difficulty to Awareness rolls.

**High-Torment Appearance**: Glow dims to sullen red. Wings become leathery, eyes black as void. Carries herself as a haughty tyrant rather than noble leader.

**High-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Claws/Teeth**: Manifests claws and fangs. Strength +2 aggravated damage.
2. **Scales**: Dark, lustrous scales cover skin. 4 dice armor vs physical attacks.
3. **Increased Size**: Body grows to 1/3 again normal height. +1 Str, +2 Dex, +1 Sta.
4. **Dread Gaze**: Those who meet demon's gaze and fail Willpower roll (diff 7) forfeit actions for turn.

## Devils (First House) Visages

### Nusku — Visage of the Flames

**Lore**: Flame | **House**: Devils

**Low-Torment Appearance**: Revealed in a blaze of yellow-orange light. Skin glows with seething brilliance of the sun, image shimmers like a mirage. Eyes of burnished gold. Hair becomes deep red or reddish-gold and thickens into a leonine mane. Open flames bow toward them.

**Low-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Shroud of Flames**: Surrounded by nimbus of supernatural flame. +1 difficulty to all ranged/melee attacks against character.
2. **Immunity to Fire**: Suffers no damage from heat or fire.
3. **Extra Actions**: Spend Faith points to gain extra actions (1 Faith = 1 action). Actions occur in descending initiative order.
4. **Improved Initiative**: Add +2 to initiative score.

**High-Torment Appearance**: Skin turns black like smoke with patterns of sullen red pulsing with heartbeat. Eyes glow like coals, thick hair writhes in spectral wind.

**High-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Claws/Teeth**: Manifests claws and fangs. Strength +2 aggravated damage.
2. **Lashing Tail**: Long reptilian tail with curved bone spike. Strength -1 aggravated damage.
3. **Increased Size**: Body grows to 1/3 again normal height. +2 Str, +1 Dex, +1 Sta.
4. **Fiery Blood**: Blood burns like magma. Flammable objects hit burst into flame; attackers in close combat suffer 1 lethal damage when they successfully damage the character.

### Qingu — Visage of Radiance

**Lore**: Radiance | **House**: Devils

**Low-Torment Appearance**: Incandescent figure wreathed in corona of jewel-like color. Physical features smooth as marble. Voice pure as crystal, cutting through the din of the mortal world.

**Low-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Wings**: Eagle's wings extend from shoulders. Glide up to 3x running speed per turn.
2. **Inhuman Allure**: Voice and features refined to inhuman perfection. +2 Charisma, +1 Manipulation, +1 Appearance.
3. **Radiant Aura**: Corona of shifting multicolored hues. +1 difficulty to ranged attacks against character.
4. **Sense the Hidden**: Supernaturally adept at sensing those who hide. -2 difficulty to Perception rolls to detect hidden individuals in line of sight.

**High-Torment Appearance**: Aura colors become muted, flaring angry reds and blues when angered. Features retain alabaster perfection but radiate cold menace and cruelty.

**High-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Voice of the Damned**: Voice seethes with inhuman hate and malice. -2 difficulty to Intimidation rolls.
2. **Casts No Reflection**: Image does not appear in mirrors, photographs, or video.
3. **Corrosive Spit**: Spit sears like acid. Bite deals +1 aggravated damage; can spit at target up to 10 feet for 1 die aggravated damage + extra successes.
4. **Horns**: Curved ram or bull's horns. If attacked in close combat, may make free counterattack (Dex + Brawl); Strength -1 aggravated damage.

## Scourges (Second House) Visages

### Dagan — Visage of Awakenings

**Lore**: Awakening | **House**: Scourges

**Low-Torment Appearance**: Mortal body infused with blush of youth and vibrant health. Even oldest vessel appears in prime of life with inhuman grace, speed and strength. Radiates warmth like sunlight. Living beings touched are temporarily suffused with restorative power.

**Low-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Aura of Vitality**: Living beings within Faith yards heal bashing damage at 1 health level per turn.
2. **Pass Without Trace**: -2 difficulty to Stealth rolls. Leaves no footprints, disturbs no foliage.
3. **Improved Physical Capabilities**: +1 Strength, +1 Dexterity, +1 Stamina.
4. **Wings**: Owl's wings extend from shoulders. Glide up to 3x running speed per turn.

**High-Torment Appearance**: Body becomes breeding ground for disease and tumors. Covered with misshapen growths and weeping sores, flesh damp and spongy to touch.

**High-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Miasma**: Breath reeks of gangrenous rot. Victims within Faith feet forfeit actions unless Stamina roll (diff 7) succeeds. Botch = disease.
2. **Extra Health Levels**: Gains 3 extra Bruised health levels.
3. **Viscous Flesh**: Diseased flesh sloughs away. +2 difficulty to grapple demon; can escape bonds with Dex roll.
4. **Extra Limbs**: Second set of arms or prehensile tail. Arms allow extra parries or 2 additional attacks per turn.

### Ellil — Visage of the Winds

**Lore**: Winds | **House**: Scourges

**Low-Torment Appearance**: Tall and lithe with large eyes and swift, graceful movements. Constantly surrounded by shifting winds that ebb and flow with emotions. Smoke or steam swirls into vortex around head and shoulders.

## Malefactors (Third House) Visages

### Kishar — Visage of the Earth

**Lore**: Earth | **House**: Malefactors

**Low-Torment Appearance**: Towering figure with dark skin (creamy brown to utter black). Body appears hewn from stone with muscle and bone in sharp relief. Hairless. Eyes have clarity and color of gemstones. Air smells of freshly turned earth.

**Low-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Increased Size**: Body grows to 1/3 again normal height. +2 Str, +1 Dex, +1 Sta.
2. **Immune to Bashing Damage**: Immune to attacks that inflict bashing damage alone.
3. **Irresistible Force**: -2 difficulty to Feats of Strength.
4. **Night Vision**: Can see in total darkness as if daylight.

**High-Torment Appearance**: Huge and misshapen, stone-like skin covered in sharp-edged nodules and spikes, fissured with deep cracks oozing black oily ichor.

**High-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Extra Limbs**: Second set of arms. Can parry without sacrificing attack, or make 2 additional attacks per turn.
2. **Gaping Maw**: Metabolism like blast furnace, consumes any material. -2 difficulty to bite attacks; Strength +4 aggravated damage.
3. **Spikes**: Sharp stony spikes cover body. +2 dice aggravated damage to unarmed attacks.
4. **Ichor**: Foul black ichor covers body. +2 difficulty to grapple attempts against demon.

### Antu — Visage of the Paths

**Lore**: Paths | **House**: Malefactors

**Low-Torment Appearance**: Closely resembles mortal at first glance. Deeply tanned skin. Dark eyes with intricate patterns radiating from eyes across face. At night, lines reflect moonlight in ghostly traceries that shift and realign.

**Low-Torment Abilities**:

**High-Torment Abilities**:
1. **Pass Without Trace**: -2 difficulty to Stealth rolls. Leaves no footprints, disturbs no foliage.
2. **Alter Size**: Can reduce size to 1/3 original volume. +2 difficulty to attacks against miniaturized demon.
3. **Mirage**: Air warps creating optical illusion. +2 difficulty to ranged attacks, +1 to close combat attacks against demon.
4. **Relentless**: Can walk or run without rest. Unaffected by fatigue or hunger as long as in motion.
