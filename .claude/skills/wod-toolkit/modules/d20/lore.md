# Lore Module — Demonic Powers

Reference for Lore and Evocations.

## Overview

Lore represents the fallen's understanding of and power over aspects of Creation. Each Lore contains five Evocations of increasing power.

**Key Mechanics**:
- Roll: Attribute + Ability (varies by evocation)
- Difficulty: Usually 6, modified by situation
- Faith spent: Add automatic successes
- High Torment: May corrupt the evocation's effects

---

## Learning New Lore

- **House Lore**: Can learn any Lore from your House
- **Common Lore**: Available to all demons
- **Other Houses' Lore**: Requires teacher from that House; 1.5× XP cost

**Finding Teachers**:
- Teachers may demand service, knowledge, or favors
- Some Lore is jealously guarded
- Earthbound may know forgotten evocations

---

## High Torment Effects

When a demon's Torment exceeds their current Faith:
- Evocations manifest in corrupted form
- Healing becomes harm
- Protection becomes imprisonment
- Inspiration becomes obsession
- Each Lore entry describes specific high-Torment effects

## Data Lookup

```bash
# Get Lore overview
python scripts/lookup.py d20.lore house-lore "Devils"

# Get specific evocations
python scripts/lookup.py d20.lore evocations "Lore of Flame"

# Search by effect
python scripts/lookup.py d20.lore evocations --find "heal"
```
