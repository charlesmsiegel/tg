# Locations Module

## Overview
Major Kindred landmarks, strongholds, and domains for chronicle setting. Use for inspiration, plot hooks, or direct inclusion. From V20 Companion Chapter Four.

---

## North America

### Gaslamp District (San Diego, California)
**Type**: Contested freeport
**Controlling Sect**: None (disputed)

**Background**: When Tara betrayed her Anarch supporters to become Camarilla Prince, the Anarchs rioted in the tourist-friendly Gaslamp District. The Sabbat from Tijuana seized the opportunity to strike.

**Current State**:
- Completely unstable nightmare
- Camarilla promises half-honored
- Anarchs nursing betrayal
- Sabbat packs sneaking in for weekend terror
- Emerged as "freeport" where any sect can clash or negotiate
- No power holds true sway

**Plot Hooks**:
- Neutral ground negotiation gone wrong
- Sabbat pack using chaos as cover
- Anarch revenge plot against Prince Tara
- Masquerade breach in tourist zone

### Lodin's Boardroom (Chicago, Illinois)
**Type**: Abandoned elder haven
**Controlling Sect**: Contested (Chicago domain)

**Background**: When Lupines rampaged through Chicago in mid-1990s, Prince Lodin declared Blood Hunt against werewolves and died at their claws. Half of Chicago's Kindred perished.

**Rumored Contents**:
- **Roll of the Damned**: Mystic "Domesday Book" listing every Kindred since Caine
- **Kabbalistic Amulet**: 13th-century, bears True Name of Brujah Methuselah Menele
- **Letter of Succession**: Grants Brujah Maxwell first right of Praxis

**Current State**:
- Heavily fortified
- Skeptics suspect "lost relics" are just tragedy rumors
- Others suspect bait in the Jyhad
- Artifacts' potential value too great to ignore

**Plot Hooks**:
- Heist to retrieve specific artifact
- Faction war over succession letter
- Ancient Methuselah awakens when True Name invoked
- Roll of the Damned contains unexpected entries

### Dr. Netchurch's Laboratory (Lowell, Massachusetts)
**Type**: Research facility
**Controlling Sect**: Independent (Malkavian)

**Background**: Malkavian bioeschatologist Dr. Douglas Netchurch studies the scientific realities of the Kindred condition. Pioneer of "posthumous sciences."

**Current State**:
- Located in unassuming business park
- Doctor squatting in unleased office space
- Receives steady shipments and "volunteers"
- Volunteers often Caitiff from Boston, Manchester, New York
- Sterile, despairing atmosphere
- During absences: starving, skeletal Cainites near frenzy
- Doctor sometimes "forgets" to provide blood

**Plot Hooks**:
- Recruited/kidnapped as test subject
- Netchurch's absence leaves experiments unsupervised
- Discovery that threatens Masquerade
- Breakthrough that changes understanding of vampirism

### Succubus Club (Nomadic)
**Type**: Traveling Kindred gathering
**Controlling Sect**: Currently Anarch-dominated

**Background**: Original appeared in Chicago early 1990s. Now nomadic party traveling North America like a carnival.

**Current State**:
- Never provides same experience twice
- Rotating promoters, acts, DJs, road crew
- Rarely stays more than one night
- Debauchery not seen since Carthage
- Elders attend despite Masquerade concerns
- Currently Anarch pulpit for the cause
- Known Tal'Mahe'Ra assassin used as cover
- Sabbat ritae sometimes incorporated
- Possibly mobile Jyhad instrument

**Plot Hooks**:
- Assassination contract during event
- Secret meeting under cover of party
- Masquerade breach at public venue
- Cross-sect romance or alliance formed
- Status raised or grudges settled

## Europe

### Prime Chantry (Vienna, Austria)
**Type**: Clan stronghold
**Controlling Sect**: Tremere

**Background**: Ultimate bastion of Tremere authority. "Called back to Vienna" instills dread in Warlocks. The Pyramid is not a loving institution.

**Rumored Features**:
- Possibly oldest surviving structure in Vienna
- City built upon it at Tremere direction
- Sprawls as widely as city itself
- Geomantic connections: under Danube, atop Millennium Tower, St. Rupert's Church towers, Hundertwasserhaus service hallways
- Gargoyle thralls shuttle equipment
- Layout changes as frequently as weekly via Thaumaturgy
- Information evolves and communicates instantly among Tremere
- Grimgroth's library location shifts regularly

**Plot Hooks**:
- Summoned for judgment
- Theft from constantly-shifting layout
- Tremere defector seeking escape
- Outsider influence detected in Vienna

---

### Catacombes de Paris (Paris, France)
**Type**: Elysium/meeting ground
**Controlling Sect**: Camarilla (Prince François Villon)

**Background**: Vast ossuary with 6+ million deceased. 300km of tunnels; only 2km open to public. Popular among Kindred despite elite disdain for "affected and obvious" aesthetic.

**Current State**:
- Prince Villon considers it Elysium
- Even Anarchs respect the catacombs
- Hosts: secret deals, Grand Guignol performances, masquerade fêtes
- **Recent Crisis**: Entire tourist group exsanguinated and arranged in horrific poses referencing Kindred courts
- Joint police/Kindred investigation ongoing
- Closure blamed on "vandalism"
- Something dwells there that hates both mortals and Kindred

**Plot Hooks**:
- Investigate the unknown predator
- Secret meeting in unmapped tunnel
- Performance that goes horribly wrong
- Discovery in sealed section

### Tomb of the Patriarch (Istanbul, Turkey)
**Type**: Historical/spiritual landmark
**Controlling Sect**: Contested (feuding factions)

**Background**: The "Dream" of Byzantium—Cainite utopia of enlightenment—was shepherded by Methuselahs over a millennium ago. The Patriarch fell to diablerie during Crusades.

**Features**:
- Replica of Hagia Sophia exists beneath the "true" Hagia Sophia
- Regarded as spiritual landmark by Byzantine elders
- Academic riches rival Library of Alexandria
- Contains verifiable history and prophecy clues

**Current State**:
- Feuding factions war over legacy
- Lies at forbidden center of hostile entente
- Kept inaccessible rather than shared
- The Dream is all but broken

**Plot Hooks**:
- Brokering peace for access
- Theft of specific text
- Faction recruits outsiders
- Discovery that reignites the Dream

## Middle East & Africa

### Alamut (Near Tehran, Iran)
**Type**: Clan stronghold
**Controlling Sect**: Assamite

**Background**: Ancestral home of Clan Assamite. Stony fortress over 1,000 years old, beneath ruins of mortal hashishin stronghold.

**Features**:
- Assassin training grounds
- Sorcerer archives
- Iran government attempting tourist development
- Sentries make undetected approach near-impossible
- The "Old Man" directs clan affairs from beneath

**Current State**:
- Rife with internal schism
- Enemies without, suspicious elders within
- Rumors: discovered Antediluvian guarded jealously
- Rival Methuselahs may vie for power in depths
- Recent pilgrimages by Assassins of all ages
- Image of radicalized strength cultivated

**Plot Hooks**:
- Pilgrimage to ancestral home
- Schism exploitation by outsiders
- Discovery in deepest archives
- Contract originating from the Old Man

---

### Pyre of Moloch (Tunisia)
**Type**: Spawning pit/horror site
**Controlling Sect**: Baali

**Background**: When Romans sacked Carthage, they found human sacrifice and infants thrown into braziers. The Punic Wars stretched the Masquerade thin—open Jyhad between Ventrue and Brujah.

**Features**:
- Hidden among Carthage ruins (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
- Avoided archaeological scrutiny
- Great brazier surrounded by tiny bones
- Streaked with blackened blood
- Buzzes with flies, crawls with maggots
- Spawning pit for Baali-aligned vampires

**Current State**:
- Timeless evil issues forth nightly
- "Kindred" spawned know songs of Pazuzu
- Still speak tongue of Moloch
- Birth nightmares, leave only desecration

**Plot Hooks**:
- Stop Baali emergence
- Archaeologist discovered too much
- Relic retrieval from ancient temple
- Trace enemy back to source

## Scandinavia

### Utgård (Vesterøy, Norway)
**Type**: Open vampire domain
**Controlling Sect**: Independent (local Kindred)

**Background**: Remote island where Kindred believe Ragnarök has passed. Named for Norse realm of giants.

**Population**:
- ~45 vampires
- ~300 mortal thralls

**Features**:
- Kindred rule openly and defiantly
- Mortals exist only to serve and feed
- Jaded, debased unlives of savagery
- Not far removed from Dark Ages jarls
- Blood-rite orgies
- Mortals believe nothing else exists
- Reverted to superstitious semi-barbarism

**Current State**:
- Close to terror of First City
- Who would believe tales of "vampire island"?
- Mortals know Ragnarök passed
- Isolation maintained by geography and belief

**Plot Hooks**:
- Escaped thrall reaches mainland
- Outsider Kindred stumbles upon domain
- Internal power struggle among vampire lords
- Archaeologist/journalist investigates legends

## Creating Custom Locations

### Elements to Define
1. **Physical Description**: Architecture, atmosphere, sensory details
2. **History**: How it became significant to Kindred
3. **Current Control**: Sect, clan, or disputed
4. **Access**: Who may enter, restrictions
5. **Secrets**: Hidden features, rumored contents
6. **Threats**: Internal or external dangers
7. **Plot Hooks**: 3-5 story seeds

### Location Types
- **Havens**: Personal or shared resting places
- **Elysiums**: Neutral meeting grounds
- **Strongholds**: Clan/sect headquarters
- **Contested Zones**: Disputed territories
- **Horror Sites**: Places of supernatural dread
- **Historical Sites**: Significant to Kindred past
- **Freeports**: Neutral ground for all factions
