# Monstrous Creations Module

Create szlachta and vozhd — war ghouls crafted by Tzimisce using Vicissitude.

**Related Modules:**
- For Blood Brothers → `modules/v20/blood-brothers.md`
- For Gargoyles → `modules/v20/gargoyle.md`

---

## Overview

The Tzimisce Clan has used Vicissitude to twist mortals into terrifying war ghouls. These creatures have lost their humanity and sanity, acting purely on instinct. They are primarily Storyteller characters and should be used sparingly.

---

## Szlachta

Named after the Polish word for "nobility," szlachta are fleshcrafted battle ghouls with armored carapaces, bone blades, and deadly spines.

### Creation Requirements
- **Vicissitude Level**: 4+
- **Time**: Months of work
- **Process**: Starvation, torture, extensive fleshcrafting
- **Blood**: Regular vitae to maintain

### Characteristics
- Barely sentient
- No free will
- Designed for combat
- Unique "signature" styling by creator

### Uses
- Haven guards and sentries
- Siege warfare (historical)
- Testament to Vicissitude mastery
- Warning to other ghouls

### Modifications
Szlachta may have various modifications:
- Bone blades (forearms)
- Armored carapace
- Spines and spurs
- Extra limbs
- Circular vision (moved eye)
- Hollow bones for lightness

### Punishment Forms
Szlachta who displease their masters may be reshaped into:
- Coat racks
- Chairs
- Lampshades
- Other furniture

Furniture szlachta retain only: Stamina, Perception, Intelligence

## Vozhd

Massive amalgamations of multiple ghouls fused together through Vicissitude and Koldunic sorcery into a single killing machine.

### Creation Requirements
- **Vicissitude Level**: 5+
- **Koldunic Sorcery**: Required
- **Ghouls Required**: 4-15+ (more = larger)
- **Blood Ritual**: All ghouls drink potion containing blood of all participants
- **Process**: Continuous chanting while sculpting ghouls together
- **Maintenance**: 5-10 blood points per month

### Characteristics
- Juggernaut of destruction
- Consumes everything in path
- Multiple limbs, mouths, eyes
- Completely mindless
- Massive Masquerade breach

### Historical Use
- Common in Dark Ages battles
- Rarely used in modern nights
- Decision to unleash never taken lightly

### Modern Considerations
- Some Fiends theorize adapting for modern warfare
- Single gigantic humanoid form proposed
- Still requires massive resources

## Vicissitude Modifications (Merit)

Player ghouls/revenants may take Vicissitude Modifications as a 1-7 point Merit.

**Requirement**: Monstrous Flaw (Appearance 0)

### Available Modifications

| Modification | Cost | Effect | Drawback |
|--------------|------|--------|----------|
| Circular Vision | 1 | Eye in back of head; bonus to rear Perception | -2 dice to depth perception/ranged |
| Fangs | 1 | Bite attack per V20 p. 276 | -2 Manipulation (non-intimidation) |
| Iron Stomach | 2 | Digest any organic material; +2 vs ingested poison | — |
| Spurs | 2 | Bone spurs; Str+2 damage | Hard to conceal; mobility issues |
| Carapace | 3 | Bony/spiky plates; +2 soak dice | — |
| Patagia | 4 | Bat-like wing membranes; gliding | -1 soak vs concussive damage |

### Healing Modifications
- Ghouls/revenants cannot heal damaged modifications naturally or with Blood
- Requires Vicissitude user with level HIGHER than original modifier

## Storytelling Notes

### Using War Ghouls
- Primarily antagonists or set pieces
- Signal serious Tzimisce presence
- Create horror and tension
- Emphasize consequences of Vicissitude

### When to Deploy
- Siege scenarios
- Defense of major Tzimisce haven
- Climactic battle scenes
- Horror emphasis

### Masquerade Implications
- Both szlachta and vozhd are massive Masquerade breaches
- Must be hidden or explained away
- Deaths attributed to accidents, animals, etc.
- Bodies must be disposed of carefully
