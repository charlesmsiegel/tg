# Mortal Character Creation Module

## Overview
Creates mortal characters: hunters, investigators, cultists, potential Embraces, or ordinary people caught in the World of Darkness.

## Mortal Types
- **Mundane**: Ordinary humans unaware of the supernatural
- **Aware**: Know vampires exist, may work with/against them
- **Hunter**: Actively hunt supernatural (True Faith, Numina optional)
- **Ghoul Candidate**: Suitable for future ghouling
- **Embrace Candidate**: Suitable for future Embrace
- **Cultist**: Serve vampires willingly (blood cults, etc.)

## Creation Steps

### Step 1: Concept & Type
Determine mortal type and role in chronicle:
- Relationship to Kindred (ally, enemy, pawn, unaware)
- Profession and social position
- What makes them notable enough to appear in story

### Step 2: Attributes (6/4/3)
**Note**: Mortals get 6/4/3 vs vampires' 7/5/3

| Priority | Dots |
|----------|------|
| Primary | 6 |
| Secondary | 4 |
| Tertiary | 3 |

### Step 3: Abilities (11/7/4)
**Note**: Mortals get 11/7/4 vs vampires' 13/9/5

| Priority | Dots |
|----------|------|
| Primary | 11 |
| Secondary | 7 |
| Tertiary | 4 |

### Step 4: Backgrounds (5 dots)
Same as vampires, but some restricted:
- **Not Available**: Generation, Herd (usually)
- **Available**: Allies, Contacts, Fame, Influence, Mentor, Resources, Retainers, Status

### Step 5: Virtues (7 dots)
- Conscience (or Conviction): starts at 1
- Self-Control (or Instinct): starts at 1  
- Courage: starts at 1
- 7 additional dots to distribute

### Step 6: Humanity & Willpower
- **Humanity** = Conscience + Self-Control (typically 6-8)
- **Willpower** = Courage (starting, can increase with freebies)

### Step 7: Freebies (21 points)
**Note**: Mortals get 21 freebies vs vampires' 15

| Trait | Cost |
|-------|------|
| Attribute | 5 per dot |
| Ability | 2 per dot |
| Background | 1 per dot |
| Virtue | 2 per dot |
| Humanity | 1 per dot |
| Willpower | 1 per dot |
| Numina | 7 per dot |
| True Faith | 7 per dot |

### Step 8: Merits & Flaws (Optional)
- Maximum 7 points of Flaws
- Some supernatural Merits available (True Faith, Numina, Medium)
- Clan-specific Merits not available

### Step 9: Numina (Optional)
Mortals may possess limited supernatural abilities:

**Hedge Magic** (not true Awakened magic):
- Paths cost 7 freebies per dot
- Common paths: Alchemy, Divination, Fortune, Healing, Herbalism

**Psychic Phenomena**:
- Powers cost 7 freebies per dot
- Common powers: Clairvoyance, Precognition, Psychokinesis, Psychometry, Telepathy

**True Faith**:
- Costs 7 freebies per dot
- Repels vampires (difficulty 10 - Faith rating to approach)
- Extremely rare (Faith 3+ almost unheard of)

## Special Mortal Types

### The Hunter
Mortals who hunt the supernatural:
- Often have True Faith or Numina
- May have Mentor (hunter organization)
- Common Abilities: Investigation, Occult, Firearms, Melee
- Suggested Merits: True Faith, Danger Sense, Iron Will

### The Cultist
Mortals who serve vampires willingly:
- May be seeking immortality
- Often blood bound to domitor
- May have Backgrounds tied to vampire (shared Resources, etc.)
- Watch for mental deterioration over time

### The Investigator
Police, journalists, private eyes:
- High Investigation, Streetwise, Law
- Contacts and Allies Backgrounds
- May become Aware through investigation
- Potential threat or asset to Kindred

## Quick NPC Generation

### Competent Professional
- Primary Attribute: 4 in relevant area
- Key Ability: 3-4 dots
- Supporting Abilities: 2-3 dots each
- Willpower: 5-6

### Average Person
- Attributes: 2s across the board, one 3
- Abilities: 1-2 in profession-related, 0-1 elsewhere
- Willpower: 3-4

### Exceptional Individual
- Primary Attribute: 5
- Key Ability: 4-5 dots
- Willpower: 7-8
- May have 1-2 dots in Numina or True Faith

## Health & Combat
- **Health Levels**: 7 (same as vampires)
- **No Regeneration**: Heal naturally (weeks to months)
- **No Blood Pool**: Cannot spend blood for anything
- **No Disciplines**: Unless Numina or ghouled
