# Anarch Glossary and Quick Reference

Quick reference for Anarch-specific terminology, titles, and concepts from V20: Anarchs Unbound.

---

## Titles and Positions

| Title | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| **Baron** | Leader of an Anarch domain exercising more or less absolute power, officially or by common acknowledgment |
| **Chancellor** | Executive of parliamentary democracy, chosen by prevailing faction. Domain may have multiple Chancellors. (Not the Camarilla Chancellor who records boons) |
| **President** | Head of executive branch in democracy with strict separation of powers |
| **Reeve** | Head of law enforcement, loosely equivalent to Sheriff but with less summary powerâ€”must seek warrants/permits for searches, seizures, and arrests |
| **Legate** | Agent of powerful Anarch domain sent to allied domains as liaison, spy, and strongarm. Less polite term: **Cheka** (after Lenin's secret police) |
| **Molotov** | Traveling agent provocateur for hire, employed to harass enemy domains while hiring domain denies responsibility |
| **Citizen** | Full member of a Free State with voting rights and domain access |

## Organizations and Groups

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Compact** | Randomly chosen group of citizens who stand collective surety for each other. Also called **frankpledge**, **cumann**, **circle**, **posse**, **krew** |
| **Free State** | Inconsistently applied term generally meaning an Anarch domain. May also mean: domain without Baron, California's Anarch Free State, or all Anarch domains collectively |
| **The Movement** | The Anarch Movement as a whole |
| **Red Question** | Mysterious radical faction spreading Anarch philosophy through enchanted digital networks |
| **Digital Draculas** | (In)famous Hacktivist coterie from Cupertino, California. Created Fangchat and other secure communication tools |

---

## Events and Customs

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **La Libertad** | Domain's anniversary celebration. In California usually Cinco de Mayo; elsewhere corresponds to local revolution date. Often includes swearing-in of new citizens and rewards for brave exploits |
| **The Rant** | Common Anarch gathering where all may speak freely |

## Technology

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **SchreckNet** | Secondary Internet built by Nosferatu, accessible only through special procedures impossible for non-Kindred. Originally clan-exclusive, now includes select outsiders |
| **Fangchat** | Secure chat program created by Digital Draculas. Freeware. Still recommends typing as though a Nosferatu were watching |

## Historical Accords

### Convention of Thorns (October 23, 1493)
- Ended the Anarch Revolt
- Anarchs joined Camarilla with full rights
- Assamites accepted Tremere curse against diablerie
- Signed near Silchester, England

### Treaty of Tyre (1496)
- Supplemental to Convention of Thorns
- Restricted Assamite territory and travel
- Required dismantling Alamut's defenses
- Camarilla agreed not to employ Assamites as assassins

### Status Perfectus
California Anarch Free State declaration of principles:
1. Freedom and independence from any organization
2. Self-rule without Prince, Primogen, or unwanted ruler
3. Kinship with oppressed Kindred everywhere
4. Responsibility to assist others in their freedom struggle
5. Commitment to maintaining the Masquerade
6. Recognition of duty to all Kindred
