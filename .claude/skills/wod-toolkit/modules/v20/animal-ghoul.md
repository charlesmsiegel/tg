## Overview

Any living creature can become a ghoul by drinking vampire blood. Animal ghouls gain:
- Extended lifespan
- Enhanced physical abilities
- Usually Potence, Fortitude, or Celerity 1
- Blood bond to domitor

---

## Creation Rules

### Blood Requirements
- Animals require less blood than humans
- Smaller animals may share blood pool (e.g., 4 bats = 1 blood point)
- Must be fed regularly or revert to normal

### Discipline Assignment
| Animal Type | Typical Discipline |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Predators (cats, wolves) | Potence or Celerity |
| Defensive (bears, gators) | Fortitude or Potence |
| Fast/Agile (bats, birds) | Celerity |
| Large (horses, elephants) | Fortitude |

### Blood Bond
- Animals can be blood bonded
- Animalism helps control but isn't required
- Bond makes animal loyal but doesn't grant intelligence

### Limitations
- Intelligence limits training
- Cannot learn complex commands
- May still respond to instincts
- Frenzy possible in combat/blood situations

## Catching Wild Animals

Some animals require Disciplines or Animal Ken to catch.

| Animal Type | Difficulty | Requirements |
|-------------|------------|--------------|
| Small bugs | Easy | None |
| Large bugs | Easy | None |
| Flying bugs | Moderate | Net or Discipline |
| Small birds/bats | Moderate | Trap or Discipline |
| Rats | Easy | Trap |
| Feral cats | Moderate | Animal Ken or Discipline |
| Wild dogs | Hard | Animal Ken or Discipline |
| Big cats | Very Hard | Discipline required |
| Bears | Very Hard | Discipline required |
