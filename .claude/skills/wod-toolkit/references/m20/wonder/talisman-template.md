**Rank:** 4 | **Arete:** 4 | **Powers:** 4 | **Quint:** 20

A jade pendant carved as a coiled serpent with ruby eyes. Created by a Euthanatos master in 16th century India, granting powers over life, death, and perception.

### Powers

**Death Sight** (Entropy 1, Life 1): See how close someone is to death. No cost.

**Serpent's Venom** (Life 3): Inflict poison (3L, progressive). Touch target. 1 Quint.

**Shed Skin** (Life 3): Heal 2L or 4B. Press to wound. 2 Quint.

**Final Rest** (Entropy 4): Grant peaceful death to willing/helpless. 3 Quint.
