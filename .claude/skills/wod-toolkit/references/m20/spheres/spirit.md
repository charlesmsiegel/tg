# Spirit (The Sphere of Ephemera)

*The Sphere governing the Umbra and spiritual entities*

## Level 1: Spirit Sight

**Capabilities:**
- See spirits, ghosts, and Umbral entities
- Perceive auras
- Sense the local Gauntlet rating
- Detect Fetishes and enchanted spirit-items
- See into the Penumbra (Near Umbra)

## Level 2: Touch Spirit/Manipulate Gauntlet

**Capabilities:**
- Touch and speak to spirits
- Raise or lower local Gauntlet rating by 1
- Call upon spiritual essence (totem connections)
- Invite willing spirits into material hosts
- Summon elemental spirits if substantial element is present

**Key Conjunctions:**
- Spirit 2 + Mind 1: Conceal your Avatar
- Spirit 2 + Life 3: Invoke totem powers (fangs, claws with spiritual essence)

**Optional Rule (Spirit-Based Focus):** If your paradigm is spiritually-rooted, add Spirit 2 to other Sphere effects when calling upon spiritual essence.

## Level 3: Pierce Gauntlet/Affect Spirit

**Capabilities:**
- Step sideways (enter the Umbra)
- Heal or harm spirits
- Awaken sleeping object spirits
- Lull active spirits to dormancy
- Call spirits through the Gauntlet
- Create ephemeral spirit-constructs (with Prime 2)

**Stepping Sideways Difficulty:** Gauntlet rating
| Location | Typical Gauntlet |
|----------|------------------|
| Deep wilderness | 4-5 |
| Rural area | 5-6 |
| Suburbs | 6-7 |
| City | 7-8 |
| Downtown/industrial | 8-9 |
| Node | 3-4 |
| Sanctum | Varies (often lower) |

**Key Conjunctions:**
- Spirit 3 + Mind 3 + Prime 2: Psychic agony against spirits
- Spirit 3 + Life 3 + Prime 2: Create living ephemeral beings
- Spirit 3 + Prime 2: Harm ghosts (alternative to Entropy 3 + Prime 2)

## Level 4: Rend/Repair Gauntlet/Bind Spirits

**Capabilities:**
- Rip holes in the Gauntlet (create spirit gates)
- Seal holes in the Gauntlet
- Bind spirits to objects or places
- Create Fetishes (spirit-powered items)
- Exorcise possessing entities
- Trap spirits in Gauntlet cages
- Lock spirits into material hosts

**Fetish Creation:**
- Unwilling spirit: Spirit 4
- Willing spirit: Prime 4

**Key Conjunctions:**
- Spirit 4 + Prime 2: Reshape spirit ephemera
- Spirit 4 + Corr 4: Open gates between worlds
- Spirit 4 + Matter 3 + Prime 2: Create golems
- Spirit 4 + Life 4 + Prime 2: Create zombies by binding ghosts to corpses
- Spirit 4 + Life 2 + Prime 2: Animate corpse with bound ghost

**Binding Successes:** Depend on spirit power:
- Minor spirit: 5 successes
- Moderate spirit: 10 successes
- Major Umbrood: 15+ successes
- Add +10 if Avatar Storm is in effect

**Summoning is Almost Always Vulgar.** Exceptions for minor spirits or special conditions.

**Capabilities:**
- Create Horizon Realms (with other Spheres)
- Enter the Deep Umbra

- Reshape spirit forms completely
- Bind the mightiest entities

**Key Conjunctions:**
- Spirit 5 + Life 5 + Mind 5 + Prime 2: Create life with souls
- Spirit 5 + Prime 5: Disintegrate spirits completely (scatter across landscape, takes long time to reform)
- Spirit 5 + Entropy 5: Destroy spirit's conceptual foundation (may never reform)
- Spirit 5 + Corr 5 + Prime 5: Create spirit prisons/Horizon Realms

**Spirit Wracking (Punishment):**
| Method | Spheres |
|--------|---------|
| Direct damage | Spirit 3 (usual), Spirit 5 (devastating) |
| Ephemeral mutation | Spirit 4/5 + Prime 2 |
| Psychic agony | Mind 3 + Spirit 3 + Prime 2 |
| Primal disruption | Prime 2 + Spirit 4 |
| Disintegration | Prime 5 + Spirit 5 |
| Entropic dispersal | Entropy 5 + Spirit 5 |
| Prolonged agony | Add Time 3 or 4 |

**Resurrection Requirements:**
- Recently dead (~5 min): Life 4 + Spirit 4 + Prime 3
- Full resurrection: Life 5 + Mind 5 + Spirit 5 + Prime 3
- Add Matter 2-4 if body has decayed
- May need Umbral travel to find departed soul

**Gilgul:** Spirit 5. Destroys another mage's Avatar permanently. Forbidden by most Traditions. Creates eternal enmity with target's allies.
