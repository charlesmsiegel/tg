## Vulgarity Guidelines

### Usually Coincidental
- All Rank 1 perception effects
- Subtle probability manipulation (Entropy 2-3)
- Healing when not witnessed
- Mind effects (invisible to onlookers)
- Minor telekinesis
- Enhancing existing conditions
- Teleporting when departure/arrival unseen
- "The Batman" (disappearing into darkness)

### Usually Vulgar Without Witnesses
- Teleportation
- Creating matter/forces/life from nothing
- Major shapeshifting
- Stepping sideways (Spirit 3)
- Most Time manipulation beyond perception

### Always Vulgar
- Rewinding time (+3 difficulty always)
- Obvious impossibilities witnessed by Sleepers
- Creating storms from nothing
- Raising the dead
- Flying without visible means
- Anything that obviously defies physics in plain view

## Time Paradox Stacking

- **Turns:** 1 layer of Paradox per turn rewound
- **Intervals:** 2 layers per interval on timeline chart
- Rewinding time is ALWAYS vulgar, even in Sanctums

## Extra Actions (Time 3)

- 2 successes per additional action
- 1-2 extra actions: may be coincidental
- 3+ extra actions: vulgar

## Repeated Time Effects

Each successive Time effect in a scene must beat the successes of the previous one (except simple perception or speed-up effects).

## Duration Chart

Successes determine how long effects last (Base Damage or Duration chart in M20 core).

## Optional: Spirit-Based Focus

If your paradigm works through spiritual connection (shamanism, totem magic, etc.):
- Add Spirit 2 to other Sphere effects when calling upon spiritual essence
- Example: Shaman growing wolf fangs = Life 3 + Spirit 2
- Reflects magic coming THROUGH spiritual connection
