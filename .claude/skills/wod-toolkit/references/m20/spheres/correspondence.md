# Correspondence (The Sphere of Connection and Space)

*Also known as: The Data Sphere (Technocracy)*

## Level 1: Immediate Spatial Perceptions

**Capabilities:**
- Sense immediate spatial relationships within vicinity
- Find hidden things nearby through spatial awareness
- Maintain perfect orientation and direction sense
- Perceive distances and spatial configurations
- Detect distortions in local space

**Common Effects:**
- Locate concealed objects or compartments
- Always know where you are relative to surroundings
- Sense when someone is watching or approaching

## Level 2: Sense/Touch Space

**Capabilities:**
- Sense distant locations you have a connection to
- Touch or affect things through space
- Teleport small objects (pocket-sized or smaller)
- Scan areas remotely
- Draw upon Quintessence from distant Periapts

**Key Conjunctions:**
- Corr 2 + Prime 2: Draw Quintessence from distant battery
- Corr 2 + Mind 3: Project telepathy across distances
- Corr 2 + Forces 2: Manipulate forces at a distance

**Common Effects:**
- Clairvoyance (basic remote viewing)
- Teleport small items to your hand
- Reach through space to touch something nearby

## Level 3: Pierce Space/Teleport Self

**Capabilities:**
- Teleport yourself to known locations
- Open/close temporary gates between locations
- View multiple places simultaneously
- Co-locate your perceptions across distances
- Connect multiple people for group communication

**Teleportation Successes:**
| Distance | Successes |
|----------|-----------|
| Within immediate perception | 1 |
| Line of sight | 2 |
| Very familiar place | 3 |
| Familiar place | 4 |
| Visited once | 5 |
| Described location | 6+ |

**Key Conjunctions:**
- Corr 3 + Forces 3 + Entropy 2: Minor weather tweaking at distance
- Corr 3 + Mind 3: Link minds across distance for group telepathy
- Corr 3 + Life 2: Teleport while maintaining body integrity

**Coincidental Teleportation:**
- Teleporting when no one can see departure/arrival
- "The Batman" technique (disappearing into darkness)
- Emerging from shadows, doorways, blind spots

**Vulgar Teleportation:**
- Vanishing in plain sight
- Appearing where you clearly couldn't be

## Level 4: Rend/Warp Space

**Capabilities:**
- Create permanent or semi-permanent gates
- Create wards against spatial passage
- Be in multiple places at once (with Life 4/Mind 2)
- Open portals large enough for groups
- Affect distant locations equal to your Correspondence rank with other Spheres

**Key Conjunctions:**
- Corr 4 + Life 4 + Mind 2: Split into multiple selves
- Corr 4 + Spirit 4: Open gates between worlds
- Corr 4 + Time 4: Create anchor points for time travel
- Corr 4 + Forces 4: Open elemental gates

## Level 5: Spatial Mutilation/Co-Location

**Capabilities:**
- Stretch or compress space permanently
- Overlay multiple locations simultaneously
- Create pocket dimensions
- Perform spatial mutilation (devastating attacks)
- Teleport unwilling targets

**Key Conjunctions:**
- Corr 5 + Mind 5 + Life 3: Control several duplicate selves
- Corr 5 + Spirit 5 + Prime 5: Create Horizon Realms
- Corr 5 + Matter 3 + Prime 2: Create multiple copies of objects
- Corr 5 + Forces 5: Weather manipulation anywhere in world
