# Demon: The Fallen Overview

Quick reference for Demon: The Fallen 20th Anniversary content creation.

## Core Concepts

- **The Fallen**: Angels who rebelled against God, imprisoned in the Abyss, now possessing mortals
- **Houses**: Seven celestial orders, each with unique Lores and purpose
- **Factions**: Five philosophical groups determining how demons interact with the world
- **Faith**: Divine energy harvested from mortals, powers evocations
- **Torment**: Corruption from the Abyss, affects powers and apocalyptic form
- **Apocalyptic Form**: The demon's true celestial appearance (one Visage per character)

## Character Creation Summary

### Attributes
- 7/5/3 distribution across Physical, Social, Mental

### Abilities  
- 13/9/5 distribution (11/11/11 after removing Dodge/Linguistics)
- No ability above 3 at creation

### Supernatural Traits
- **Lore**: 3 dots across House Lores (primary determines Visage)
- **Faith**: 3
- **Torment**: Based on House (Devils/Scourges: 3, others: 4)
- **Willpower**: 3

### Other
- **Backgrounds**: 5 dots
- **Virtues**: 7 dots across Conscience, Conviction, Courage
- **Freebies**: 15 points
