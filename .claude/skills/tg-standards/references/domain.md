## Universal Stats

- **Attributes** - Physical/Social/Mental (rated 1-5)
- **Abilities** - Skills, knowledges, talents (rated 0-5)
- **Backgrounds** - Character advantages
- **Willpower** - Mental fortitude (rated 1-10)

## Vampire Terms

- **Clan** - Vampire lineage
- **Generation** - Distance from original (lower = stronger)
- **Blood Pool** - Vitae reserve
- **Disciplines** - Vampiric powers

## Werewolf Terms

- **Tribe** - Werewolf nation
- **Auspice** - Moon phase role
- **Breed** - Birth form (Homid/Metis/Lupus)
- **Gifts** - Spiritual powers
- **Rage** - Fury pool
- **Gnosis** - Spiritual awareness

## Mage Terms

- **Tradition/Convention** - Magical faction
- **Spheres** - Nine magical domains
- **Arete** - Magical enlightenment
- **Quintessence** - Magical energy
- **Paradox** - Reality backlash
- **Resonance** - Magical signature

## Wraith Terms

- **Legion** - Wraith faction
- **Shadow** - Dark side personality
- **Angst** - Shadow's power
- **Pathos** - Emotional energy
- **Fetters** - Ties to living world
- **Arcanoi** - Ghostly powers

## Changeling Terms

- **Kith** - Fae type (Sidhe, Pooka, etc.)
- **Court** - Seelie or Unseelie
- **House** - Noble lineage
- **Glamour** - Faerie magic
- **Banality** - Mundane reality
- **Arts/Realms** - Magic system

## Demon Terms

- **House** - Angelic order
- **Faction** - Demonic allegiance
- **Lores** - Fallen powers
- **Faith** - Mortal worship energy
- **Torment** - Corruption level
