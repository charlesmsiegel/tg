# Grimoire Merits and Flaws

## Merits

| Merit | Cost | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Accelerated Rote Learning** | 4 pt | Rotes particularly well explained. Study rolls to learn rotes rolled twice, use better result. |
| **Adaptive Format** | 2 pt | Text magically reorganizes to ease learning. +1 die to all study rolls. |
| **Advanced Insights** | 5 pt | More advanced than power indicates. Can teach one trait to Rank+1. |
| **Founder's Legacy** | 6 pt | Written by legendary faction founder. Study rolls cannot botch. |
| **Harmonic Resonance** | 4 pt | Resonance adapts to reader. When casting contained rote using the Grimoire, difficulty -1. |
| **Living Tome** | 5 pt | Living repository wanting to be understood. Per trait studied: +2 dice to study roll OR convert one 1 to a 10. |
| **Master's Annotations** | 4 pt | Marginalia and commentary. +1 automatic success on any study roll. |
| **Mnemonic Encoding** | 3 pt | Abilities magically encoded. Ability study rolls gain 1 automatic success. |
| **Palimpsest** | 4 pt | Written over older decipherable texts. Perception + Academics (diff 8) allows rerolling up to Rank failed dice. Botch dice rerolled give Paradox. |
| **Reality Anchor** | 3 pt | Virtually impossible to change. Even time tampering leaves it unedited. |
| **Sanctified Text** | 2 pt | Protected against corruption. When casting contained rote, increase Paradox Backlash difficulty by 1. |
| **Universal Translation** | 2 pt | Translates into reader's preferred language. |

## Flaws

| Flaw | Gain | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Contagious** | 3 pt | Infects readers with obsession. Causes derangement until stopped and two successful Willpower rolls (diff 8) in a row, rolled nightly. |
| **Corrupted Text** | 5 pt | Works subtle corruption. Upon completion, character gains Corrupted Resonance. |
| **Cursed** | 1-5 pt | Causes problems for owner. Severity per M20 p. 646. |
| **Fragmentary** | 4 pt | Grimoire is damaged. One trait can only be learned to Rank-1. |
| **Hungry Tome** | 6 pt | Refuses to yield secrets easily. -2 dice to study rolls, 1's count as 2 botches, gain Paradox. Preventable by bribing with Quintessence (1 per Rank, matching Resonance). |
| **Maddening Labyrinth** | 5 pt | Botching a study roll immediately drops mage into Quiet derived from contents. |
| **Massive Work** | 3 pt | Generally immobile. Study must happen at Grimoire's location. |
| **Nightmarish Text** | 3 pt | Causes nightmares. Day after studying, Willpower (diff 7) or -1 dice penalty next day. |
| **Obscure Orthography** | 1 pt | Difficult to parse. Study rolls at -1 die unless reader has Academics 3+. |
| **Paradigm Lock** | 3 pt | +1 difficulty for characters not of authoring faction. |
| **Restricted Access** | 1-3 pt | Only readable under conditions. 1pt: simple (night only). 2pt: precise (blood moon after sacrifice). 3pt: very specific (13th full moon of leap year, virgin reader). |
| **Single Source of Truth** | 1 pt | Cannot study same trait from another Grimoire or Mentor simultaneously. |
| **Soul-Tainted Ink** | 4 pt | Made from human sacrifice. Studying gives dot of Resonance matching author. |
| **Temporal Anomaly** | 3 pt | Contains future knowledge. Every study roll gives Paradox and triggers backlash roll. |

**Maximum Flaw Points: 7**
