## Member Profile Format

```markdown
### [Title] [Name]
**[House/Sub-faction]** | **Arete [X]** | **[Sphere] [X], [Sphere] [X], [Sphere] [X]**

[2-3 sentences describing role, personality, appearance, and history. Include links to any locations, grimoires, rotes, or items they're associated with.]

**Sanctum**: [Link if they have one]
**Notable Items**: [Links to personal items]
```

### Example

```markdown
### Magister Scholae Octavian Cross
**House Bonisagus** | **Arete 5** | **Prime 5, Mind 4, Correspondence 3**

The Librarian Prime and overseer of the [Athenaeum](../libraries/athenaeum.md). A distinguished scholar in his late sixties with silver hair and spectacles, Octavian has spent four decades cataloging and preserving Hermetic knowledge. He developed [The Silver Conduit](../rotes/silver_conduit.md) rote and personally maintains the [Time Garden](../locations/time_garden.md).

**Sanctum**: [Tower Study](../sanctums/tower_study.md)
**Notable Items**: [Octavian's Scrying Mirror](../items/personal/octavians_scrying_mirror.md)
```

## Retainer Guidelines

### Sorcerers (Linear Mages)

Practitioners of hedge magic using Paths instead of Spheres.

**Common Paths:**
- Alchemy — Potions and transformations
- Divination — Seeing past/future/hidden
- Enchantment — Creating magic items
- Fortune — Luck manipulation
- Healing — Wounds and illness
- Hellfire — Fire/energy attacks
- Mana Manipulation — Working with Quintessence
- Shadowcasting — Darkness manipulation
- Shapeshifting — Changing form
- Summoning/Binding/Warding — Spirit work

**Profile Format:**
```markdown
#### [Name]
**Role:** [Job Title] | **Paths:** [Path 1] [Rating], [Path 2] [Rating] | **Practice:** [Practice]

[2-3 sentences including background, personality, connection to chantry. Links to relevant locations or members.]
```

### Consors (Skilled Mortals)

Non-magical allies with valuable mundane skills.

**Common Roles:**
- Library staff (catalogers, archivists, translators)
- Security (guards, investigators, drivers)
- Household (housekeepers, cooks, groundskeepers)
- Medical (physicians, nurses)
- Administrative (accountants, lawyers, assistants)
- Specialized (veterinarians, art conservators, tech specialists)

**Profile Format:**
```markdown
#### [Name]
**Role:** [Job Title] | **Specialty:** [Skills]

[2-3 sentences including background, personality, connection to chantry.]
```

## Cross-Linking Requirements

Every member profile should link to:
- Their sanctum (if they have one)
- Items they own or created
- Locations they're associated with
- Grimoires or rotes they authored
- Other members they have relationships with

Link back from those files to the member profiles.
